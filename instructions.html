Instructions

Dear participant , please read the instructions before the experiment.

Experiment Overview

In this experiment you will be asked to key in either continuous or separate sentences, paragraphs and phrases using both AutoComPaste and traditional techniques. After the whole experiment, an csv file will be generated to capture your typing speed for each trial.


Practice Trials

Before the main experiment, there will be two practice trials to let you be familiarized with this two techniques.

For traditional technique, you can just find the text indicated on the left in the right article box. The text will be highlighted for easier searching.


For AutoComPaste

  1. The remaining words will be shown when you type three characters or more.
  2. Press ⬆ ⬇ buttons will scroll through the possible words. Press enter or click to choose.
  3. After choosing, press ← or → right after pressing enter, you can delete/append the next sentences. Press enter means pasting the final text.

After you finishing the practice trials, you can see a pop up window. Click “ok” to enter the main experiment.


Main Experiment


The main experiment contains 3 blocks. Each block will have 12 conditions. Each condition will have 4 trials to minimize the effects from some random data. An example of one block arrangement is illustrated below.


The time from you enter one trial until you click “Next Task” will be recorded as your performance for this trial. These data will help us to analyse these two techniques.

After finishing each block, there will be a pop up window to let you have a break. Press “ok” and the “Next Text” button to continue.


After Experiment

After finishing all 3 blocks, an csv file will be generated. Please download this file and send to lizanmaggie@gmail.com for further analysis. All the data will be kept confidential.
